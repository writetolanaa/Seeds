🎮 RPG SYSTEM EXPLANATION: SDG 1 (No Poverty) – Helping Others
🌍 Purpose of This Chapter
SDG 1 is the first hardship of the RPG.
The player is introduced to the world by helping poor NPCs, learning that poverty is a system problem, not a personal failure.
The design reflects real development ideas promoted by the United Nations.
1️⃣ What Poverty Means in the Game
Poverty is not one stat. It is a combination of problems:
Lack of income
Poor health
Low education
Unstable or informal work
No protection during crises
NPCs remain poor unless multiple issues are addressed together.
2️⃣ Player Role
The player is not a hero with weapons.
They are a community helper / organizer.
Their power comes from:
Connecting NPCs to opportunities
Making strategic choices with limited resources
Improving systems, not individuals only
3️⃣ NPC Behavior & Progression
Each NPC has:
Basic needs (food, health)
Long-term needs (education, skills, stable work)
If basic needs are unmet:
NPCs cannot work or study
Progress stops
If only short-term help is given:
NPC survives
Poverty continues
If long-term help is added:
NPC becomes stable
NPC can help others later
NPCs change visibly:
Unemployed → worker → skilled worker
Child out of school → student → apprentice
4️⃣ Community System (Very Important)
The game tracks community health, not just individuals.
Examples:
If many NPCs get jobs → local economy improves
If many children attend school → future jobs unlock
If illness spreads → unemployment rises
The player wins the SDG 1 chapter by raising community stability, not personal wealth.
5️⃣ Player Decisions Matter
The player must balance:
Emergency aid vs long-term solutions
Helping one NPC deeply vs helping many slightly
Spending now vs investing for later
Wrong choices don’t cause “game over” — they show consequences.
6️⃣ Education Through Gameplay (Not Text)
The game teaches by:
Showing NPC outcomes
Using dialogue, not lectures
Locking progress unless root causes are addressed
Example lesson:
Feeding people helps today.
Education and fair jobs end poverty long-term.
7️⃣ End of SDG 1 Chapter
The poverty chapter ends when:
Most NPCs have stable income
Children are back in school
The community can survive small crises without collapsing
This unlocks:
New areas
New systems (health, education, equality)
The next SDG hardship
