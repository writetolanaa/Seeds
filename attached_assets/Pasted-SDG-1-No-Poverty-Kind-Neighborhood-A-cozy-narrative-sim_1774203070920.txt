SDG 1 – No Poverty

Kind Neighborhood
A cozy narrative simulation where every small decision matters. Players help a struggling neighborhood recover by creating jobs, improving housing, and supporting families through difficult moments. The city slowly transforms as people regain stability, dignity, and hope.

⸻

SDG 2 – Zero Hunger

Grow & Serve
A vibrant farming and trading game where players grow crops, harvest produce, and sell food directly to citizens. Each citizen has unique needs, budgets, and tastes. Smart planning prevents waste and ensures that everyone in the city eats well.

⸻

SDG 3 – Good Health & Well-Being

Oops! Hospital!
A light-hearted hospital management game where health problems are caused by everyday habits—stress, poor sleep, or too much junk food. Instead of only treating symptoms, players improve lifestyles, redesign public spaces, and promote wellness across the city.

⸻

SDG 4 – Quality Education

My Real School
A realistic school simulation where players manage classes, teachers, and student life. Beyond grades, players support mental health, creativity, and inclusion. A successful school is measured by confident, curious students—not just test scores.

⸻

SDG 5 – Gender Equality

Switch the Shoes
A story-driven game where players live the same world through different genders. Opportunities, dialogue, and challenges change depending on who you play. By redesigning systems and breaking unfair rules, players reshape society into one that treats everyone equally.

⸻

SDG 6 – Clean Water & Sanitation

Water Stories
A narrative environmental game following rivers from mountains to cities. Oil spills, waste, and neglect poison the water. Players clean pollution using absorbent tools, filtration systems, and smart planning—watching life return downstream with every choice.

⸻

SDG 7 – Affordable & Clean Energy

Energy Untangle
A calming puzzle game where tangled energy networks power entire cities. Players untangle and connect solar panels, wind turbines, and water systems without energy loss. Clean, efficient solutions light up neighborhoods and reduce environmental damage.

⸻

SDG 8 – Decent Work & Economic Growth

Company Coach
A decision-driven simulation where players advise companies on how to grow responsibly. Fair wages, safe workplaces, and balanced schedules compete with profit pressure. Long-term success comes from healthy, motivated workers—not exploitation.

⸻

SDG 9 – Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure

Invent It!
A playful engineering sandbox where players design bridges, transport systems, and factories using limited resources. Every invention is tested in real time. Creative thinking and smart design lead to resilient, future-ready infrastructure.

⸻

SDG 10 – Reduced Inequalities

Little Big World
A calm, pastel-colored strategy game where tiny citizens live very different lives. Players gently adjust access to transport, education, and healthcare. As inequality shrinks, the world becomes more connected, colorful, and alive.

⸻

SDG 11 – Sustainable Cities & Communities

Soft City
A slow, relaxing city builder focused on human well-being. Players replace traffic with bikes, add green spaces, and design walkable neighborhoods. Cities grow quieter, cleaner, and happier as sustainability takes root.

⸻

SDG 12 – Responsible Consumption & Production

Reuse Please!
A creative crafting game where waste is never useless. Players sort materials and transform trash into furniture, tools, and art. The goal is a circular economy where creativity replaces overconsumption.

⸻

SDG 13 – Climate Action

Climate Diary
A long-term decision game where players shape the future through policies and everyday actions. The world evolves across decades—storms intensify or fade, ecosystems recover or collapse—based entirely on player choices.

⸻

SDG 14 – Life Below Water

Ocean Cleanup Crew
An underwater exploration game where players clean polluted seas. Removing plastic, oil, and abandoned fishing nets restores coral reefs and marine life. The ocean visibly heals as ecosystems return to life.

⸻

SDG 15 – Life on Land

Forest Friends
A peaceful nature-restoration game where players protect forests and wildlife. Replant trees, guide animals, and stop environmental damage through smart planning. The land slowly flourishes into a thriving ecosystem.

⸻

SDG 16 – Peace, Justice & Strong Institutions

Talk It Out
A dialogue-focused strategy game where words matter more than force. Players resolve conflicts, fight corruption, and build trust between communities. Peace is achieved through understanding and fairness.

⸻

SDG 17 – Partnerships for the Goals

Together Mode
A cooperative multiplayer experience where players take on different roles—governments, NGOs, businesses—and must work together to solve global challenges. No single player can succeed alone.
