SDG 9 – Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure: Invent It! – Full Coder-Ready Design

Genre: Strategy / Puzzle / Innovation Simulation
Target Audience: Teens, casual players, creative learners
Tone: Fun, creative, inventive, problem-solving

⸻

1. Core Gameplay Loop
	1.	Player receives challenges or problems from cities, industries, or communities.
	2.	Design and build innovative solutions: machines, bridges, factories, or renewable systems.
	3.	Test prototypes and optimize them for efficiency, cost, and sustainability.
	4.	Deploy solutions and measure impact on infrastructure and society.
	5.	Track innovation metrics: efficiency, sustainability, and community satisfaction.
	6.	Repeat with progressively complex challenges.

⸻

2. Step-by-Step Player Actions

Step 0: Start Challenge
	•	Display scenario: problem type (transport, energy, industrial, disaster mitigation).
	•	Show resources: budget, materials, tools, and tech options.
	•	Highlight constraints: time, space, environmental impact, safety.

Step 1: Design Solution
	•	Player selects materials and components to assemble a prototype:
	•	Structural elements (beams, supports)
	•	Functional devices (motors, gears, solar panels)
	•	Infrastructure elements (bridges, roads, water systems)
	•	Drag-and-drop interface allows placement and connection of parts.

Step 2: Test Prototype
	•	Activate simulation:
	•	Measure efficiency (energy use, material efficiency)
	•	Measure safety (stability, durability)
	•	Measure sustainability (resource consumption, emissions)
	•	Identify weaknesses or failures in the design.

Step 3: Optimize Design
	•	Adjust materials, components, or layout to improve performance.
	•	Repeat testing until thresholds for efficiency, safety, and sustainability are met.

Step 4: Deploy Solution
	•	Place final solution into the scenario world.
	•	Observe real-time impact: traffic flow, energy delivery, industrial efficiency, or disaster mitigation.
	•	Update metrics: city satisfaction, production efficiency, environmental impact.

Step 5: End Challenge
	•	Score based on: efficiency, sustainability, budget adherence, innovation creativity.
	•	Unlock next challenge with higher complexity and new materials/technologies.

⸻

3. Key Variables & Objects

Component Attributes
	•	Type: structural, functional, energy, transport
	•	Material: wood, metal, concrete, renewable components
	•	Efficiency rating
	•	Durability / safety rating

Scenario Attributes
	•	Problem type: transport, energy, industrial, disaster mitigation
	•	Constraints: budget, space, environmental regulations, time
	•	Success thresholds: efficiency %, safety %, sustainability %

Global Variables
	•	Innovation score (cumulative creativity and success)
	•	Budget remaining
	•	Resources inventory
	•	Challenge number / difficulty

⸻

4. UI Elements
	•	Top bar: challenge number, budget, efficiency score, sustainability score
	•	Component panel: materials, tools, machines
	•	Scenario view: area to place and connect components
	•	Test simulation button: visual feedback on performance
	•	Alerts: failures, budget overspend, unsafe designs
	•	End-of-challenge report: efficiency, safety, sustainability, creativity score

⸻

5. Feedback Systems
	•	Visual: components animate during testing, fail or succeed visibly
	•	Audio: construction sounds, alarms for failures, completion chimes
	•	Metrics update dynamically: efficiency, safety, sustainability
	•	Real-time scenario animations: traffic flows, energy delivery, structural integrity

⸻

6. Progression
	•	Unlock advanced components: motors, renewable energy tech, smart infrastructure
	•	Increase complexity: larger projects, multiple constraints, simultaneous problems
	•	Introduce environmental challenges: emissions limits, resource scarcity, disaster risks
	•	Track cumulative innovation score to unlock awards or bonus projects

⸻

7. Win/Loss Conditions
	•	Win: Prototype meets or exceeds efficiency, safety, and sustainability thresholds, stays within budget, and positively impacts the scenario
	•	Loss: Prototype fails testing, exceeds budget, or harms community/environmental metrics
